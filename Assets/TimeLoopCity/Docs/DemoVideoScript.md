## Technical Settings for Recording

### Camera
- FOV: 60° (cinematic)
- Depth of Field: Enabled (f/2.8)
- Motion Blur: Subtle (0.2)

### Post-Processing
- Bloom: 0.15 intensity
- Tonemapping: ACES
- Color Grading: +10 temperature (warm tropical)
- Vignette: 0.2 intensity

### Lighting
- Time of Day: 17:00 (golden hour)
- Sun Angle: 25° elevation, -45° azimuth
- Volumetric Fog: 0.003 density

### Performance
- Resolution: 1920x1080
- Target: Locked 60 FPS
- Record: 4K @ 60fps, downscale in post
