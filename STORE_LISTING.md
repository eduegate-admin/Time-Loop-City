### Long Description
**The City is Dying. You have 5 Minutes to Save It.**

*Time Loop City* is a narrative-driven mystery game where time is your greatest enemy and your only ally. Stuck in a perpetual 5-minute cycle, you must explore a living, breathing metropolis, uncover dark secrets, and piece together the puzzle of the apocalypse before the world resets.

Every loop is a new chance. Learn the schedules of the city's inhabitants, discover hidden clues, and unlock new dialogue paths. What you learn in one life carries over to the next. Can you find the Truth and break the cycle?
