### Long Description
**The Queen of the Arabian Sea is Dying. You have 5 Minutes to Save Her.**

*Time Loop City: Kochi* is a narrative-driven mystery game where time is your greatest enemy and your only ally. Stuck in a perpetual 5-minute cycle, you must explore the vibrant streets of **Kochi, Kerala**, from the colonial history of **Fort Kochi** to the bustling promenade of **Marine Drive**.

Every loop is a new chance. Learn the schedules of the auto-rickshaw drivers, discover hidden secrets in the backwaters, and piece together the puzzle of the impending monsoon apocalypse. Can you find the Truth and break the cycle before the waters rise?
